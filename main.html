Paris, France

Paris, the City of Lights, is a romantic and vibrant destination known for its rich history, art, culture, and fashion.


Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower: A must-see iconic landmark offering breathtaking panoramic views.
  2. Louvre Museum: Home to world-famous masterpieces like the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre Dame Cathedral: A stunning Gothic cathedral with intricate architecture.
  4. Arc de Triomphe: A historic monument honoring French military victories.
  5. Palace of Versailles: A magnificent royal palace and gardens.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants: Flaky pastries enjoyed for breakfast or a snack.
  • Baguette: A long, crusty bread perfect for sandwiches.
  • Escargot: Snails cooked in garlic butter.
  • Steak frites: A classic French dish of steak with fries.
  • Crepes: Thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory ingredients.
